5 Things You Should Know About Zero Balance Savings Accounts:

  1. There are no minimum-balance commitments in a zero balance savings account. So, you can spend even the last rupee in your account without worrying about penalties.
  2. You can open a digital zero balance account online from the comfort of your home. All you need is your PAN and Aadhaar number.
  3. Earn the prevailing interest on your zero balance savings account deposits.
  4. With your zero balance account, enjoy facilities such as debit cards, cheque books, internet banking and mobile banking.
  5. Open a zero balance savings account instantly through Aadhaar OTP verification. Then, complete the full KYC process of your account through in-person verification within 180 days from account opening by visiting your nearest bank branch or booking an appointment.

IMPORTANT: Please ensure that you complete your Full KYC within 180 days from account opening to enjoy uninterrupted services at all times.

If Full KYC is not completed within 180 days from account opening, your account will be blocked from transactions. However, you would still be able to complete your Full KYC and resume banking easily.

If Full KYC is not completed within 365 days from account opening, your account will expire permanently with no possibility of reactivation.
